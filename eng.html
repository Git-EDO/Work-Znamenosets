«Gonfalonier».
On War.

The channel performs scholarly film-studies on warfare, the armed conflicts and fighting, weaponry, structure and employment of forces, military theory and leaders. The films are authored by scholars who enrich their studies with a range of directly relevant visual facts and constructions. The channel devotes its video-research model to integrating visual facts with the documentary sources and research techniques that are traditionally in use. The channel is committed to the rigorous review of the studies and high requirements for the authors to produce the content of the high academic class.

The studies of the channel’s founder, a historian Vladimir Shirogorov, Beria. Class Surge. 1899–1939. A personality, special operations, socialism, and Ukrainian War. The Armed Conflict over Eastern Europe in the 16th–17th centuries open the first season of the channel. The Beria. Class Surge series is the biography of the Soviet statesman and secret police leader close to Josef Stalin, Lavrentiy Beria. It reveals the fundamental social current of the USSR’s history in conjunction with Beria’s personal life flooded with the struggle between the Bolshevik clans, special intelligence operations, political terror and individual drive. The Ukrainian War series discovers Eastern Europe and its heartland, how-a-day Ukraine, in the epoch of nation-building and state-construction in the 15th – 17th century. It was a period of the endemic war over the partition and hegemonic domination in Eastern Europe when the modern nations of Poland, Russia, Sweden, Turkey, Ukraine, the Baltic and the Black Sea states were born, and the successors of the super-empire of the Golden Horde were destroyed. 

Gonfalonier’s studies are good for thinking about the past. And they help to understand the people who are making history now.

Beria. Class Surge. 1899–1939.
A personality, special operations, socialism.

Beria is the crown-prince of the Soviet epoch for its admires and the devil for its foes. One’s view of Lavrentiy Beria is determined by one’s perception of the USSR. Both scholars and dilettantes who turn to the case of Beria, - which is huge, deep and diverse in the now-a-day public opinion, - find a lot of intimate, private and individual in his person. Nevertheless, first of all, Beria is the political and social phenomenon, the essence of Soviet socialism. Beria, -smart, violent, self-minded as he was, - looks like the inborn captain of the social confrontation. He was one of a few Soviet executives who were capable to exploit the nuclear energy of the social confrontation in favour of the classes to which they committed themselves and for their power-ascendance.

Ukraine, Amphibious Aphrodite.

The film is the story of the birth of the modern Ukrainian nation in the 15th–17th centuries. Today Ukraine is one of the top issues of international policy, especially in Europe; Ukraine has its legends besides the policy, such as the beautiful Ukrainian women or Ukrainian tragedies of the 20th century. Despite the omnipresence of the “Ukrainian theme” in the news, talks and dating websites, Ukraine is still an enigma because it remains a “shadow figure” in the historical conscience of Europe. It seems that it sprang almost from nowhere thirty years ago to become the newsmaker and hot personal experience. Meanwhile, there are episodes of the Ukrainian past that are out of the boring linear history. They are attractive and telling a lot of that fascinating nation.

A series in English with five episodes. The release in 1 January 2023.
